Our Mission
SpinnyWFC aims to restore online functionality for Nintendo DS titles and select Wii titles by providing an alternative, community‑driven Wi‑Fi service. We focus on DS support first — matchmaking, rankings, level sharing, and classic WFC features — then expand to Wii titles as a lightweight alternative to existing services.
